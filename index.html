Info

Robert R. Lazar Middle School is a public middle school located in Montville Township, New Jersey. The community of Robert R. Lazar Middle School is committed to providing students with the highest quality education, which includes academic success, enhanced self-esteem, and respect for individual differences, thus creating in adolescents both a strong foundation and desire for continued learning. We teach sixth, seventh, and eighth grade. Our core values are pride, empathy, respect, cooperation, and citizenship.

Campus

Our campus is designed as an environment suitable for learning. Students arrive at the school and enter into their homeroom, where attendance will be taken. Once homeroom is over, students will head to their morning classes. In the middle of the day, lunch is eaten at the cafeteria. After lunch, students will proceed to their afternoon classes, then will be dismissed as necessary. After school, students may join clubs, which are meetings that take place dedicated to a single subject/topic. The classes taken by students are Math, Science, English Language Arts, Social Studies, Physical Education, Language (French, Italian, Spanish, or Mandarin), and an elective of their choice (Band, Choir, Orchestra, Art, etc.).
